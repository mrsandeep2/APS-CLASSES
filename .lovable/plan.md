
# APS Classes — Premium Coaching & Admission Website

## Overview
A modern, responsive website for APS Classes by Munna Sir, serving dual purposes: coaching classes (Nursery–12th) and college admission assistance. Dark blue + white + light accent color palette with Poppins/Inter typography.

## Pages & Sections

### Single-Page Website with Smooth Scroll Navigation

1. **Header** — Sticky navbar with logo "APS Classes By Munna Sir", navigation links (Home, Coaching, Admission, About, Contact), mobile hamburger menu, and a "Contact Us" CTA button.

2. **Hero Section** — Split layout with two cards side by side (stacked on mobile):
   - Left: Coaching focus with title, subtitle (CBSE & Bihar Board), "Join Coaching" button
   - Right: Admission focus with university names, "Apply Now" button
   - Subtle gradient background with animated entrance

3. **About Section** — Brief intro about APS Classes, experience, results, and Munna Sir's vision. Clean layout with an accent highlight.

4. **Highlights/Stats Section** — Animated counter cards: 500+ Students, 100+ Admissions, Experienced Faculty, Personalized Guidance. Fade-in on scroll.

5. **Coaching Section** — Cards for class groups (Nursery–10th, 11th–12th) showing streams, mediums, and boards. "View Courses" CTA.

6. **Admission Section** — University cards (LPU, Galgotias, Parul, TMU) + services list (Course Guidance, Admission Support, Career Counseling). "Apply for Admission" CTA.

7. **Testimonials Section** — Carousel/grid of student and parent reviews with names and context.

8. **Contact Section** — Two-column layout: contact info (address, phone, email) on one side, lead generation form (Name, Phone, Service Interest dropdown) on the other.

9. **Footer** — Quick links, contact details, social media icons, copyright.

## Design & UX
- **Colors**: Dark blue (`#1e3a5f`), white, light blue accent
- **Typography**: Poppins for headings, Inter for body
- **Animations**: Scroll-triggered fade-ins, hover effects on cards/buttons, smooth scroll navigation
- **Mobile-first**: Hamburger menu, stacked layouts, touch-friendly CTAs
- **Performance**: Minimal dependencies, optimized images, clean component structure

## Components
- Reusable `SectionHeading`, `StatCard`, `ServiceCard`, `UniversityCard`, `TestimonialCard` components
- Contact form with validation (name, phone, service interest)
- Mobile-responsive navigation with sheet/drawer menu
